Downloading tinData with maven

<dependencies>
    ...
        <dependency>
                <groupId>net.micwin.tindata</groupId>
                <artifactId>tindata-core</artifactId>
                <version>0.2.1</version>
        </dependency>
        ...
</dependencies>

Download manually

  • current binaries jar is located at http://repo1.maven.org/maven2/net/micwin/tindata/tindata-core/0.2.1/tindata-core-0.2.1.jar
  • current sources jar is located at http://repo1.maven.org/maven2/net/micwin/tindata/tindata-core/0.2.1/tindata-core-0.2.1-sources.jar
  • current javadoc jar is located at http://repo1.maven.org/maven2/net/micwin/tindata/tindata-core/0.2.1/tindata-core-0.2.1-javadoc.jar
