Downloading tinData

  • in your pom.xml, declare a repository
    <repositories>
        ...
        <repository>
                <id>tindata</id>
                <url>http://micwin.github.com/tinData/repository</url>
        </repository>
        ...
</repositories>

    I'm working hard on getting tinData to maven central.

  • then, declare a dependency 
    <dependencies>
    ...
        <dependency>
                <groupId>net.micwin.tindata</groupId>
                <artifactId>tindata-core</artifactId>
                <version>0.2-SNAPSHOT</version>
        </dependency>
        ...
</dependencies>
